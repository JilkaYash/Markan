Company Overview

Who We Are

MARKAN is a leading supplier of professional welding equipment, consumables, and accessories based in Rajkot, Gujarat, India. With over 15 years of experience, we have built a reputation for delivering high-performance welding solutions trusted by India's top industrial organizations.

Our comprehensive product range includes MIG wires, flux cored wires, hard facing wires, mild steel welding rods, cutting and grinding discs, flap discs, fiber discs, wire brushes, and a wide array of welding accessories. Every product is carefully sourced and quality-tested to ensure it meets the demanding requirements of modern industrial welding.

We proudly serve clients across agriculture, automobile, safe & locker manufacturing, machinery, forging & casting, construction equipment, renewable energy, and export & distribution sectors.
